HISTORY OF SAINT JUDE PARISH SCHOOL

In the Latte part of 1980's, Trece Martires City, the capital of Cavite Province had several elementary and secondary schools, but no Private Catholic School to call its own. Only few parents caUld afford to send their children to the nearest private catholic school that is twelve kilometers away from the city itself. These parents believe in the values of private catholic education so they clamored for the opening of St. Jude parish School. With this demand, the Parish Priest motivated and brought up a catholic institution. The vision had begun to take form.

In 1990, the elusive realization came about through the meticulous hardwork and undying pursuit of the vision. St. Jude Parish School was named after St. Jude Thaddeus, the patron saint of the hopeless cases and the patron of city itself. It was the brainchild of Fr. Nonilon B. Tibayan, the Parish Priest of St. Jude Thaddeus Parish Church.

The project did not come as a boon on a platter. It get tough especially at the start. The classroom then was made of pawid and othe scrap materials. Mrs. Aide P. Ilagan and Mrs. Bessie C. Mondragon were the prime movers of the school handling kindergarten classes with two four-year old and thirty six five-year old pupils enrolled.

A few years after the foundation of the school, improvements of the classroom tooks place. Additional classroom was built and more faculty members were hired to satisfy the needs of growing number of enrolling students. In 1994, when the first elementary course was completed, the teachers' skills were tested when the first grade six graduation was held in march 1995.

On the following school year, a first high school class was put up; Mrs. Aida P. Ilagan was positioned as the pioneer principal of the budding institution. But this time, the secondary course had reached up to the third level. On the same school year, the main building was constructed through the daring effort of School Director, Fr, Teodoro B. Bawalan.

In 1998, Rev Fr. Melencio Sandoval became the school Director and the Principal of the school. A part-time academic consultant the person og Mrs. Angelisa B. Francisco was hired to oversee the academic enhancements of the students. The first high school graduation was held on the same school year. Her term as principal lasted intil S.Y. 1999-2000 with the total of five personnel, twenty three teachers and six hundred sixty four students.

June 2000 marked another change in the growing institution with the introduction of new principal in the person of Miss Estelita J. Tibayan with Rev. Fr. Melencio Sandoval as the School Director. In the coming months, more improvements in the school system were implemented. Development in physical facilities was emphasized in the school's stipulation both in the academics and in the curricular force.

The need to continue the development scheme begun through some reformations. The expansion of the facilities and the procurement of added equipments were seen. Additional two academic classrooms, Science and Technology and Home Economice Laboratories were built. In the light of academics, some changes have taken place; to name, section become saint names, homogenous and heterogeneous sectioning, averaging to cumulative grading system, and revision of student's handbook.The students have been honed as they complete in and out campus contest. Administrative post were reshuffled and five new teachers added bloom to the entire faculty staff.

The holy retreat for the year marked a great accomplishment for the teachers and staff when they formulated the school's vision mission. Procurement of six playground units for NKP pupils was also perceived. discerning courses become easy as the school conducted Career-Orientation Seminar to graduating fourth year high school students.

In August 2004, the directorship was passed to Rev. Fr. Marino P. Limbag. The tricle of transformations become flood in May 2004, whwn Mrs. Ma. Sabina A. Manguling took charge.

For the first few months, the SJPS had undergone with school facelift. The school buildings, classrooms and fence were repainted. The administration focused on the transparent management - in physical, in financial, and in administrative facets. One of the turning points of SJPS' subsistence was when SJPS become Fund Assistance for Private Education-Education Cotracting Sevice (FAPE-ESC) Scholarship grantee. This is a four-year scholarship offered to fifty not so well-off but deserving graduating grade six pupils. On the other hand, the teachers have been continiously honed through In-Service Trainings and Seminars. Through raffle Draw-fund Raising Project, the Computer Laboratory was set up with two airconditioning unit in august 2005. The air conditioned Computer Laboratory seemed small with 30 windows XP computers. It has 2 linkjet printers, and all-in one printer. All computers in offices and faculty rooms were also connected through network.

In February 2006, the School Vision-Mission was revised and simplified during IN-SET in Makati. During 15th Foundation Anniversary, SJPS mounted another exigent activity-Fund Raising Concert featuring Bamboo ang His Band.

S.Y. 2005-2006 was welcomed with an added blast of modifications.The Chool Canteen through its own fund tiled, painted and renovated making extreamly pleasing tp the student-patrons. From the proceeds of Mr. and Ms. SJPS, the 2nd and 3rd floorof the main building with the modern technologyand to provide the students the best they deserve, the school acquired Risograph and Konica 1410 Photocopier. Through the effort of the Computer Teachers and the Principal, Virtual Library is soon to be used by the Judeans to make reaserch easy and fun.

On the continious years of untiring and dedicate service, Sjps has combined feircely several predicaments and has strive to serve the Thaddeans and the community. Trying innovative ways to be more competitive and advance in shaping not just the intellectual side but the whole aspect of all its students.

Still in the year 2007 that the new administration building was built. The said facility features the different workspaces wherein the administration staff can properly execute their respective jobs the same way that they can best serve the client-parents and the students. A spacious conference room was also constructed within the broad administration buliding.

Offices were made air-conditioned the same with the NKP room. The function school stage visibly attested the continious progress of this institution. It is where outside school-programmed activities are carried out, consequently this becomes more accesible to both students and parents-gurdians.

New batch of skilled-faculty members were hired to educate the icreasing number of students. Teacher-assistants were hired to assist the NKP advisers in handling tjeir pupils. To uplift the faculty and staff English Speaking Fluency, a work-shop seminar on English Proficiency was brought to them through the English Magister as its organizer. The teaching force, the non-teaching and even the maintenance staff were made to undergo the training that will enhance their english speaking ability.

October 2007 markoked another groth on the part of the school, a sanctified Grotto and a depict of Garden of Getsemane were put up and blesses to further hearten the students to be faithful and prayerful to God and to Mama MAry. Another great achievement spotted the school history, it was when the noble contribution of Mrs. Cecilia Erni, the owner of the land wherein St. Jude Parish Church and the School itself are presently situated, was made known to public through officialy making the school building named after her.

As it reached its 18th year, SJPS with all of its guiding principles inspiringly motivates everyone to continue strive more, formulate new brilliant ideas, survive dificulties, come up with fair decisions and set things in their right perspective, thus, satisfying the people around that truly, St. Jude Parish School is a school of hope and excellence through holistic formation.

CADIPSA VISION-MISSION

VISION

We the schools of the Diocese of Imus Cavite envision the holy and faithful people of God for a just and grace Filipino society towars a peaceful and renewed humanity. With faith in God, love for the church, interior goodness, solidarity and meritocracy.

MISSION

We commit ourselves to life long and relevant education that is Christ-centered, patriotic and evangelizing to form a whole human person, unite every family, care for the needy, protect mother earth and engage in the apostolic and ministries of the church guided by Mary Our Lady of the Pillar.

SCHOOL VISION-MISSION

VISION

Saint Jude Parish School is be an educational institution that is cradle of catholic education honing graduates to be responsive at the sigh of the times.

MISSION

We commit ourselves to:

  • provide quality catholic education with excellence guided by religion as the core of the curriculum;
  • develop and inculcate societal values to student, faculty, and staff; and
  • ensure that our institution geared toward holistic formation
